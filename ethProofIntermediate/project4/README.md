# DEGEN SIM'S ONCHAIN
 Welcome to the first online sim city.

 ## Description

 Welcome to our sim world, you can get a sim of random characteristics (age, gender, height, race and social status) absolutely free.
 In game assets can be purchased with DGN tokens or found while exploring our open world
