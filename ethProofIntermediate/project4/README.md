# DEGEN SIM'S ONCHAIN
 Welcome to the first online sim city.

 ## Description

 Welcome to our sim world, you can get a sim of random characteristics (age, gender, height, race and social status) absolutely free.
 In game assets can be purchased with DGN tokens or found while exploring our open world

 ## ABOUT DEGEN TOKENS

 DEGEN tokens will launch in 2025 with a market cap of 10 million.

 users can purchase luxury bags and shoes using DGN tokens at afforable rates.

 DEGEN token is an ERC20 token. Users will be allowed to purchase, redeem and burn their DGN tokens

 early adopters will also receive a free DGN drop
